Email: nguyent2792 [AT] gmail [DOC] com

I am currently an Associate Research Fellow and was a PhD candidate of the Applied AI Institute at Deakin University. Prior to that, I was a researcher at Ulsan National University of Science and Technology (UNIST) from Mar 2018 to Dec 2019, and I obtained my Master at UNIST in 2018.

The goal of my research is to develop a better understanding and design more efficient data-driven machine learning methods for learning and decision-making in modern practical settings for robustness, scalability and statistical efficiency. My research interest lies at the intersection of machine learning with statistics and optimization, with the key highlights:

  • Reinforcement learning (e.g., offline policy learing, bandits, distributional RL)

  • Robust generalization beyond i.i.d. setting (e.g., OOD generalization, adversarial learning)

  • Information Bottleneck

I publish under Thanh Tang Nguyen (old name arrangement) and Thanh Nguyen-Tang (new one). Here are my latest CV, Google Scholar, Semantic Scholar, Github, Twitter .

I created the “ML Theory Exchange Network” Discord channel (currently 66 members as of 25 Oct 2021) to connect ML-theory passionate self-learners (like myself) with senior researchers for exchanging ideas and learning resources.

I have been also serving as a ML technical consultant for a local startup company on NLP product solutions.
