Highlight


Attention from Fixations: The model learns to attend to the salient regions in the videos from eye fixations.

Pedestrian Detection: The region proposal network directly predicts boxes of various scales at each position in an image.

PIB: Each layer in DNNs is considered as a bottleneck that splits the architecture into an encoder and a variational relevance decoder. Compression and relevance are then induced at each bottleneck.

Reading Comprehension: The network answers to a question relevant to a context by selecting a span in the context. The question might or might not answerable.
