Deakin University, Australia
Email: nguyent2792 [AT] gmail [DOC] com

I am currently an associate research fellow and a PhD candidate of the Applied AI Institute at Deakin University. Prior to that, I was a researcher at Ulsan National University of Science and Technology (UNIST) from Mar 2018 to Dec 2019, and I obtained my Master at UNIST in 2018.

The goal of my research is to develop a better understanding and design more efficient data-driven machine learning methods for learning and decision-making in modern practical settings. My research lies at the intersection of machine learning with statistics and optimization.

  • Offline Reinforcement Learning (theory) with neural function approximation

  • Robust generalization beyond i.i.d. setting

  • Distributional Reinforcement Learning

  • Information Bottleneck for robust representation

  • Causal Inference for Robust Generalization

I publish under Thanh Tang Nguyen (old name arrangement) and Thanh Nguyen-Tang (new one). Here are my latest CV, Google Scholar, Semantic Scholar, Github, Twitter .
